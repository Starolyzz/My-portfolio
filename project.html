01 — Context

A studio in search of a universe

The brief was deceptively simple: create a brand that felt ambitious without being arrogant. A system that could hold complexity — multiple service lines, diverse audiences — without losing coherence.

The challenge wasn't standing out. It was standing for something. We started by identifying the studio's gravitational core — the one idea everything else orbits.

Brand Exploration — Early Concepts
02 — Process

From concept to constellation

The process began with a deep audit of the studio's existing touchpoints — what they'd inherited, what they'd outgrown. Sketches followed research. Dozens of directions narrowed to three, then one.

The final mark draws from geometric principles: a simple form that rewards closer looking, revealing new relationships between its parts at every scale.

"Design is not just what it looks like and feels like. Design is how it works — and for whom."

— Design principle guiding this project

03 — Outcome

A system that scales

The final brand system shipped with a comprehensive guidelines document, a full suite of print collateral, and digital templates the studio could deploy independently from day one.

Six months post-launch, the studio reported stronger client recognition and used the new identity to land two of their largest accounts to date.
