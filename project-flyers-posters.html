The Work

Two modes: client work and personal work.

This collection spans two distinct contexts. The car meet flyers were client work — event promotional materials designed for real audiences, with real deadlines and distribution across both print and social. The personal posters were self-directed: a DPR Ian album redesign exploring psychedelic art as a historical design form, a Spider-Man Daily Bugle broadsheet, and a New Balance ad — made to push craft, explore reference, and keep the eye sharp.

The Outcome

Work that proves range.

The car meet flyers went to print and were distributed across social — reaching a real event audience. The personal posters exist as proof of range: the ability to move between high-energy event graphics and considered, reference-led editorial design without losing craft in either direction.

Personal projects are where you find out what you actually care about as a designer. Client work is where you prove you can apply it.
